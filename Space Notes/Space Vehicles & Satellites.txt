Space Vehicles & Satellites Topology with Inspirations  
--------------------------------------------------------
Human Spaceflight Vehicles  
├── Early Spacecraft  
│   ├── Vostok (USSR, first human in space) → Inspired by R-7 ICBM  
│   ├── Mercury (USA, first American in orbit) → Based on Redstone & Atlas missiles  
│   ├── Gemini (USA, two-person crew, EVA, docking) → Evolution of Mercury  
│   ├── Voskhod (USSR, multi-crew, first spacewalk) → Upgraded Vostok  
│  
├── Moon Missions  
│   ├── Apollo Command Module (USA) → Derived from Gemini, Saturn V launched  
│   ├── Apollo Lunar Module (USA) → First true extraterrestrial lander  
│  
├── Space Shuttle Era  
│   ├── Space Shuttle (USA) → Inspired by Dyna-Soar, reusable orbiter  
│   ├── Buran (USSR) → Soviet response to Shuttle, automated flight  
│  
├── Modern Crew Vehicles  
│   ├── Soyuz (USSR/Russia, ISS transport) → Inspired by Vostok & Voskhod  
│   ├── Orion (USA, deep-space crew vehicle) → Inspired by Apollo, larger capacity  
│   ├── Crew Dragon (SpaceX) → Based on Dragon cargo vehicle  
│   ├── Starliner (Boeing) → Commercial crew capsule, similar to Apollo  
│   ├── Starship (SpaceX) → Fully reusable, inspired by Shuttle & Saturn V  
│  
Robotic Spacecraft & Rovers  
├── Early Robotic Missions  
│   ├── Luna (USSR, first Moon impact & soft landing) → Inspired by ICBM designs  
│   ├── Ranger (USA, first Moon impact attempts) → Precursor to Surveyor  
│   ├── Surveyor (USA, first soft landing on Moon) → Pathway for Apollo  
│  
├── Mars Rovers  
│   ├── Sojourner (USA, first Mars rover) → Small experimental design  
│   ├── Spirit & Opportunity (USA, long-duration rovers) → Inspired by Sojourner  
│   ├── Curiosity (USA, nuclear-powered) → Based on Spirit/Opportunity  
│   ├── Perseverance (USA, sample-return tech) → Upgraded Curiosity design  
│  
Satellite Systems  
├── Early Satellites  
│   ├── Sputnik 1 (USSR, first satellite) → Based on R-7 ICBM  
│   ├── Explorer 1 (USA, first US satellite) → Based on Juno rocket  
│  
├── Navigation Satellites  
│   ├── Transit (USA, first navigation satellite) → Inspired GPS system  
│   ├── GPS (USA, global positioning system) → Successor to Transit  
│   ├── GLONASS (Russia) → Soviet counterpart to GPS  
│   ├── Galileo (EU) → European GPS alternative  
│  
├── Communication Satellites  
│   ├── Telstar (USA, first active relay satellite) → Inspired by Echo passive sat  
│   ├── Intelsat series (Global) → Evolved from early comsats  
│   ├── Starlink (SpaceX, LEO internet) → Mega-constellation model  
│  
├── Earth Observation Satellites  
│   ├── Landsat (USA, Earth imaging) → Inspired by early reconnaissance satellites  
│   ├── GOES (USA, weather monitoring) → Based on early NOAA sats  
│   ├── Sentinel (EU, environmental monitoring) → Successor to Envisat  
│  
├── Deep Space Probes  
│   ├── Pioneer (USA, first interplanetary probe) → Inspired by early Earth satellites  
│   ├── Voyager (USA, deep space exploration) → Based on Pioneer design  
│   ├── Cassini-Huygens (USA/ESA, Saturn mission) → Built on Voyager success  
│   ├── New Horizons (USA, Pluto flyby) → Evolved from Voyager & Galileo  
│   ├── Parker Solar Probe (USA, Sun study) → Inspired by Helios & Ulysses
