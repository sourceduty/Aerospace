SpaceX Vehicle and Engine Hierarchy

SpaceX Vehicles
  ├── Falcon 9 (Reusable, 2-stage)
  │   ├── First Stage (Merlin 1D engines)
  │   └── Second Stage (Merlin Vacuum engine)
  ├── Falcon Heavy (Reusable, 3-stage)
  │   ├── Boosters (Merlin 1D engines)
  │   └── Second Stage (Merlin Vacuum engine)
  └── Starship (Fully Reusable, 2-stage)
      ├── Super Heavy Booster (Raptor engines)
      └── Starship Upper Stage (Raptor engines)
          └── Starship Engines (Raptor engines, full-scale)
