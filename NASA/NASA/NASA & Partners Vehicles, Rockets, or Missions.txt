NASA and Partners Vehicles, Rockets, and Missions

NASA and Partners
  ├── NASA
  │   ├── Space Launch System (SLS)
  │   │   ├── Block 1 (RS-25 engines, Solid Rocket Boosters)
  │   │   ├── Block 1B (RS-25 engines, Exploration Upper Stage)
  │   │   └── Block 2 (RS-25 engines, Exploration Upper Stage, additional boosters)
  │   ├── Orion Spacecraft
  │   │   ├── Crew Module (for astronauts)
  │   │   └── Service Module (ESA provided)
  │   ├── Artemis Missions
  │   │   ├── Artemis I (uncrewed lunar mission)
  │   │   ├── Artemis II (crewed lunar flyby)
  │   │   └── Artemis III (crewed lunar landing)
  │   └── Mars Mission (Perseverance Rover, Ingenuity Helicopter)
  │       ├── Perseverance Rover (Mars exploration)
  │       └── Ingenuity Helicopter (Mars aerial exploration)
  ├── SpaceX (Partner)
  │   ├── Falcon 9 (Reusable, 2-stage)
  │   │   ├── First Stage (Merlin 1D engines)
  │   │   └── Second Stage (Merlin Vacuum engine)
  │   └── Crew Dragon Capsule (for crewed missions to ISS)
  ├── Boeing (Partner)
  │   ├── CST-100 Starliner (Crewed and cargo missions)
  │   └── Atlas V Rocket (for Starliner launches)
  ├── Lockheed Martin (Partner)
  │   ├── Orion Spacecraft (with NASA)
  │   └── Mars Missions (partnership in spacecraft development)
  └── Northrop Grumman (Partner)
      ├── Cygnus (Cargo missions to ISS)
      └── Antares Rocket (launches Cygnus spacecraft)
